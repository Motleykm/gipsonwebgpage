Ways of Travel in Delaware

Philadelphia International Airport (PHL)

Location: Approximately 30 miles north of Wilmington, DE, in Philadelphia, PA.

Description: Philadelphia International Airport is the primary airport serving the Greater Philadelphia area. It offers a wide range of domestic and international flights, making it a convenient option for Delaware travelers.

Facilities: The airport features multiple terminals, a variety of dining and shopping options, car rental services, and shuttle services to Delaware.

Transportation to Delaware: Travelers can use shuttle services, rental cars, or take SEPTA Regional Rail to Wilmington, DE.

Website: Philadelphia International Airport

Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport (BWI)

Location: Approximately 70 miles southwest of Wilmington, DE, in Baltimore, MD.

Description: BWI is a major hub for domestic flights and offers some international services. It is known for its convenient access to both Baltimore and Washington, D.C.

Facilities: BWI has extensive parking, rental car services, numerous dining and shopping options, and free Wi-Fi.

Transportation to Delaware: Options include rental cars, shuttle services, or taking the MARC train to connect with Amtrak services to Wilmington.

Website: Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport

New Castle Airport (ILG)

Location: Located in Wilmington, DE.

Description: New Castle Airport is a smaller regional airport that primarily serves private and corporate aircraft. It occasionally offers commercial flights.

Facilities: The airport has limited passenger amenities but provides essential services such as car rentals and parking.

Transportation: Travelers can rent a car or use local taxi and ride-sharing services to reach their destination.

Website: New Castle Airport

Amtrak Train Station

Location: Wilmington, DE.

Description: The Wilmington Amtrak Station is a key hub for train travel in Delaware, offering connections to major cities along the Northeast Corridor, including New York, Philadelphia, Baltimore, and Washington, D.C.

Facilities: The station features a ticket office, waiting areas, restrooms, and limited dining options. It also provides parking facilities and car rental services nearby.

Transportation: The station is easily accessible by local transit, taxi, and ride-sharing services. Passengers can also connect to SEPTA Regional Rail for local travel.

Website: Amtrak Wilmington Station

DART First State

Description: DART (Delaware Transit Corporation) operates bus services throughout the state, providing an affordable and convenient option for local travel.

Services: DART offers fixed-route buses, paratransit services, and seasonal beach bus services.

Connections: DART buses connect to key locations such as the Wilmington Amtrak Station, shopping centers, hospitals, and other points of interest.

Website: DART First State

SEPTA Regional Rail

Description: SEPTA (Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority) operates Regional Rail services that extend into Delaware.

Routes: The Wilmington/Newark Line connects Philadelphia to Wilmington and Newark, DE.

Transportation: This service provides a reliable option for commuters and travelers between Delaware and Philadelphia, with stops at multiple stations in Delaware.

Website: SEPTA Regional Rail
